## Profit Withdrawal

Withdrawal conditions:

<Steps>
  <Step title="4 profitable days">
    Accumulate 4 profitable days (each with ≥ 1% growth)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Active subscription">
    Subscription is active
  </Step>
  <Step title="Profit over HWM">
    Unrealized profit exists over the high-water mark
  </Step>
</Steps>

The withdrawal arrives at the trader's EVM address on HyperEVM. Proportion covers gas for the withdrawal transaction — the trader does not need to pay.

After withdrawal, the floor resets to account size and trading continues.

<Info>
The trader can cancel the subscription — on the next renewal date, the FA closes.
</Info>

## FA Closure

The account closes on:

- Trailing / daily drawdown breach
- Max leverage breach
- Trading a non-allowed market
- Unpaid subscription

<Warning>
On closure, capital and all profit return to the LP vault. To receive their profit share, the trader must request a withdrawal before a closure trigger fires.
</Warning>
