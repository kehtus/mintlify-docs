The central element of Proportion is the Vault contract. All protocol liquidity flows meet there: LP deposits, funded-account financing, USDC custody, fees, and profit distribution. Everything that moves capital in the system passes through Vault.

## Shares and sharePrice

An LP's share in Vault is represented by shares. Accumulated profit automatically increases `sharePrice` — yield does not need to be claimed separately.

```
sharePrice = totalAssets / totalShares
totalAssets = USDC on EVM + USDC allocated to active FAs
```

## Allocation Limits

| Parameter | Value | What it limits |
|-----------|-------|----------------|
| Max utilization | **80%** | Share of Vault allocated to active FAs |
| Max single account | **10%** / $20k | Size of one FA relative to `totalAssets` |
| Builder leverage | **10x** | Max builder funding relative to `sharesValue` |

## Lock-Up

LPs without a builder role can withdraw shares at any time. Builder shares are locked while they have active FAs. `withdrawableShares()` shows how many shares are available to withdraw.

## Governance

Vault parameters (`feeMultiplier`, `MIN/MAX_ACCOUNT_SIZE`, `MIN/MAX_DRAWDOWN_BPS`, `BUILDER_LEVERAGE`, utilization, per-FA limits) are changed by the admin through setters.
