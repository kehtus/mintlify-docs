## Limits

| Rule | Description | Violation |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| **Trailing drawdown** | Drawdown limit from the peak. Profit is locked into the floor once per day | FA closure |
| **Daily drawdown** | Daily limit, equal to 50% of trailing. Enabled by the prop firm at activation | FA closure |
| **Max leverage** | 5x notional/equity | FA closure |
| **Market whitelist** | Trading is allowed only on liquid perps from the protocol list | FA closure |
| **Subscription** | 1.5%/month of account size | FA closure |

<Info>
At activation, the prop firm sets: account size, drawdown %, and whether daily DD is enabled.
</Info>

## What Is Allowed

| Activity       | Status   |
|----------------|----------|
| Copy Trading   | Allowed  |
| Bots & EAs     | Allowed  |
| News Trading   | Allowed  |
| Information-based Trading | Allowed |

<Tip>
Everything not forbidden on this page is allowed. Trading style, trade frequency, position size, holding overnight or through weekends — no restrictions within the listed limits.
</Tip>
