## What the Builder Chooses at Activation

| Parameter | Range | What it does |
|-----------|-------|--------------|
| `trader` | any address | Who receives the FA |
| `accountSize` | $10–$20,000 | Capital size (USDC, 6 dec) |
| `drawdownBps` | 5%–25% | Trailing drawdown limit |
| `dailyDrawdownEnabled` | bool | Enable daily limit (=50% of drawdownBps) |

`MIN/MAX_ACCOUNT_SIZE` and `MIN/MAX_DRAWDOWN_BPS` are configurable by the protocol admin.

## Allocation Limits

| Parameter | Value | What it limits |
|-----------|-------|----------------|
| Max utilization | **80%** | Share of LP vault allocated to active FAs |
| Max single account | **10%** / $20k | Size of one FA relative to `totalAssets` |
| Builder leverage | **10x** | Total accountSize of builder FAs ≤ `sharesValue × 10` |

`getMaxAccountSize()` and `builderAvailableFunded(builder)` show current limits.

## Activation Fee (One-Time)

```
fee = accountSize × drawdownBps × feeMultiplier / 1_000_000
```

`feeMultiplier = 120` (1.2x risk premium) is the default. The parameter is mutable: the protocol admin sets it through `setFeeMultiplier()` within 1.0x–2.0x. If `dailyDrawdownEnabled = true` — 20% discount.

| accountSize | drawdown | dailyDD | Fee |
|-------------|----------|---------|-----|
| $5,000 | 5% | OFF | $300 |
| $5,000 | 10% | OFF | $600 |
| $5,000 | 10% | ON | $480 |
| $10,000 | 25% | OFF | $3,000 |
| $20,000 | 10% | OFF | $2,400 |

Distribution: 90% → LP vault, 10% → protocol. Includes the first month of subscription.

The builder includes this amount plus their own margin in the challenge price for the trader.

## Subscription Fee

```
fee = accountSize × subscriptionFeeMultiplier / 10000
```

`subscriptionFeeMultiplier = 150` → 1.5% of accountSize per month. Charged every 30 days. The trader can cancel (`cancelSubscription`) — at the next checkpoint in the renewal zone, the FA closes.

Distribution: 85% builder, 15% protocol.

## Profit Withdrawal

When the trader requests a withdrawal (`requestWithdrawProfit`):

<Steps>
  <Step title="Profitable days">`profitableDaysCount ≥ 4` (4 days of ≥ 1% growth)</Step>
  <Step title="Subscription">Subscription is active</Step>
  <Step title="Profit over HWM">Profit exists over the high-water mark</Step>
  <Step title="Splits">80% trader, 13% LP vault, 7% protocol</Step>
  <Step title="Reset">After withdrawal, HWM resets to accountSize and profitable days reset</Step>
</Steps>

## FA Closure

Triggers (all lead to `initiateClose()`):

- Trailing/daily drawdown breach
- Max leverage breach
- Trading an unauthorized perp
- Subscription unpaid in the renewal zone
- `initiateCloseAdmin()` from the relayer

After closure, positions are unwound, USDC returns to the LP vault, and builder shares unlock.
