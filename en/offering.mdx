## Problems Proportion Solves

- **Opaque rules and payout blocks** in classic prop — Proportion encodes rules and splits in code, with automatic payouts
- **B-book conflict of interest** — builders and the protocol earn from trader profit, not trader losses
- **Counterparty risk** — the funded account is non-custodial, and a prop firm physically cannot arbitrarily deny a payout outside protocol rules
- **High barrier to launching a prop firm** — Proportion provides capital, risk engine, and settlement out of the box, without forcing builders to run their own stack
- **Fragmented onchain capital** — one LP vault instead of separate pools for every firm
- **No DeFi access to prop-industry economics** — Vault shares are a composable primitive for LPs
