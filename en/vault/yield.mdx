## Yield Sources

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Activation fees" icon="percent">
    **90%** of every FA issuance goes to Vault
  </Card>
  <Card title="Profit split" icon="chart-line">
    **13%** of trader profit at the time of withdrawals
  </Card>
  <Card title="Unused activation-fee buffer" icon="shield-check">

  </Card>

<Info>
Subscription fees do not go to Vault — they are builder revenue (85%) and protocol revenue (15%).
</Info>

## Risks

Placing capital in Vault involves several risks that affect share value and LP yield.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Correlated drawdowns" icon="waves">
    The activation fee is designed to improve LP expected value in isolated events. With simultaneous breach closures across many funded accounts, aggregate losses may exceed the pool's accumulated buffer, reducing `sharePrice` — potentially below deposit nominal value.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Issuance quality" icon="users">
    LPs do not participate in trader selection or activation parameter decisions — these decisions are made by builders. Deterioration in underwriting quality by any builder affects Vault yield proportionally to their share of active FA issuance.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Smart-contract risk" icon="file-code">
    MainVault and FundedAccount contracts are exposed to critical vulnerability risk. The codebase undergoes independent audit, but residual risk cannot be eliminated.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Dependency on Hyperliquid and Hypercore" icon="link">
    Vault operations rely on Hypercore data (balances, prices) and Hyperliquid spot/perp infrastructure. Failures or incorrect behavior in these components are reflected in Vault capital.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Price slippage during liquidation" icon="zap">
    In extreme volatility, a trader's position may realize a loss below the trailing floor before the protocol initiates FA closure. The premium included in the activation fee partially absorbs this difference, but does not guarantee full coverage.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Limited liquidity for builders" icon="lock">
    Shares of participants with an active builder role are locked while they have active funded accounts. Full withdrawal is possible only after all FAs are closed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Governance risk" icon="settings">
    Vault configuration (`feeMultiplier`, allocation limits, drawdown parameters) can be changed through administrative functions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
