![Proportion overview](/images/image.jpeg)

Proportion is prop-trading infrastructure built on Hyperliquid, bringing together three sides: traders get access to capital through funded accounts without KYC or arbitrary payout blocks; builders launch their own prop firms on top of ready-made infrastructure — capital, an on-chain risk engine, and automated settlement are available out of the box; LPs deposit USDC into a shared vault and earn from activation fees and a share of trader profits.

## Context

Hyperliquid and the builder codes ecosystem created market demand for capital in onchain trading — the same dynamic that produced thousands of prop firms on forex markets. Now it is moving on-chain. This is the beginning of the onchain prop meta.

Unlike a classic prop firm, an onchain prop firm must be non-custodial, secure, verifiable, and composable. Proportion covers these requirements — it is an infrastructure layer for an onchain prop firm without the need to write custom contracts or a risk engine.

## Mission

Become the liquidity hub for onchain prop firms — a composable DeFi layer on HyperEVM that covers the capital allocation needs between traders, builders, and LPs.
