> Capital and strong execution on Hyperliquid — without KYC or artificial rules.

Proportion gives traders access to capital through onchain funded accounts on Hyperliquid: deep HL liquidity, no KYC, no hidden conditions, no tricky restrictions — a simple, clear way to get capital.

FA is a full A-book solution: the trader makes real trades with real capital and real Hyperliquid liquidity, with every stage verifiable on-chain. Unlike most prop firms in the market, the prop firm and the protocol are aligned with the trader's profit — their revenue grows with the trader, not instead of the trader.

<Tip>
The trader owns their profit share within protocol rules — protocol rules prevent the prop firm from arbitrarily denying payouts.
</Tip>

## How to Get a Funded Account

Funded accounts are issued by prop firms running on Proportion. Each one has its own approach: challenge, evaluation, or direct activation for a selected trader — this is part of the prop firm's product.

FA is activated on the trader's EVM address on HyperEVM. Until profit withdrawal, the trader does not interact with the contract — all operations are performed by the protocol. No gas payment is required.

After activation, the FA belongs to the trader — the protocol itself monitors all rules.
