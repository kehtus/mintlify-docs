---
title: "Protocol Rules"
description: "Funded-account rules enforced on-chain by the protocol"
---

The protocol enforces all funded-account rules itself. The builder cannot soften or bypass them — only choose parameters within the allowed range.

## Hard Rules

Hardcoded and shared by everyone:

| Rule | Value |
|------|-------|
| Max leverage | 5x notional/equity. Breach → positions close |
| Profit split | 80% trader / 13% LP / 7% protocol |
| Profitable days for withdrawal | 4 days with ≥ 1% growth each |
| Checkpoint interval | 1 day |
| Subscription period | 30 days |

## Trailing Drawdown (HWM-Based)

FA uses a trailing floor: HWM updates at the daily checkpoint, and the trader's profit is locked in via the floor at end-of-day. Intraday, the floor does not jump with PnL — but at the next checkpoint it ratchets up if the day closed in profit.

```
accountSize = $10k, drawdown = 10%
Start:    HWM=$10k, floor=$9k
+$5k (checkpoint): HWM=$15k, floor=$13.5k
-$2k ->    balance $13k < $13.5k -> close
```

After a profit withdrawal, HWM resets to accountSize.

## Daily Drawdown (Optional)

Enabled by the builder at activation. Daily limit = 50% of trailing drawdown.

| Trailing | Daily |
|----------|-------|
| 5% | 2.5% |
| 10% | 5% |
| 25% | 12.5% |

Checked at every checkpoint and permissionlessly through `initiateClose()`. Enabling it gives a 20% discount on the activation fee.

## Perp Whitelist

Trading is allowed only on liquid perps from the protocol whitelist. The keeper monitors positions off-chain and, when it detects an unauthorized asset, calls `initiateClose()`. The contract verifies the violation itself and closes the account if it actually happened.

## What This Means for Builders

Builders implement their own additional rules (profit target, minimum trading days, stricter daily limit) off-chain as part of their challenge and evaluation. When the trader passes the builder's criteria, the builder opens a funded account through the SDK, and from that point the protocol protects capital by its own rules.
