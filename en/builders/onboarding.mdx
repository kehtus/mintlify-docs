## Dual Role and Skin in the Game

- The builder is both product operator (FA issuance, challenge monetization) and LP (shares work across the whole pool)
- Shares are locked while the builder has active FAs
- Builder leverage 10x: total accountSize of all active FAs ≤ `sharesValue × 10`
- If shares value falls below the minimum ($10k) on withdrawal, the builder is automatically deactivated (`BuilderDeactivated`) and cannot activate new FAs
- The builder pays an activation fee for every activation (risk compensation for the LP vault)

<Warning>
A $10k+ deposit is not collateral that returns unchanged. It is working LP capital: it earns from activations and trader profits across the protocol; drawdown from traders who blow up reduces pool yield. Upside and downside are proportional to the builder's share in the vault.
</Warning>

## Trader Onboarding

When a builder activates an FA, the account is linked to the trader's EVM address on HyperEVM. The trader does not need to hold HYPE in the wallet: the protocol covers gas for all operations, including profit withdrawal. In theory, the trader may not interact with onchain infrastructure until the moment they withdraw funds — all technical operations are performed by the protocol.

This simplifies the builder's product: the trader pays for a challenge through the builder storefront, passes evaluation, and receives an FA — without needing to understand gas, wallets, or signing.
