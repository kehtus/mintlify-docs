Proportion is prop-trading infrastructure built on Hyperliquid.

It coordinates three sides of the market on a unified protocol layer. Traders access capital through funded accounts on Hyperliquid. Builders launch prop-trading products with capital, risk enforcement, and settlement already built in. LPs deposit USDC into a shared Vault and participate in activation fees and trader profit.

## What is Proportion?

Proportion brings the prop-firm model on-chain. The same demand for trading capital that produced thousands of prop firms in forex markets is now appearing around Hyperliquid and builder codes.

Unlike a classic prop firm, an onchain prop firm needs non-custodial accounts, transparent rules, verifiable settlement, and composable capital. Proportion provides these components without requiring each builder to write custom contracts or operate a risk engine.

## Technical Overview

Proportion is built around a shared Vault and isolated funded accounts. The Vault holds LP capital, allocates USDC to funded accounts, receives activation fees, and settles trader profit splits. Funded accounts trade on Hyperliquid while protocol rules enforce drawdown, leverage, market access, subscriptions, and withdrawals.

## Mission

Proportion aims to become the liquidity hub for onchain prop firms: a composable DeFi layer on HyperEVM for capital allocation between traders, builders, and LPs.
