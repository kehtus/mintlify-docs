> Capital and strong execution on Hyperliquid, without KYC or artificial rules.

Proportion gives traders access to capital through onchain funded accounts on Hyperliquid. The account trades against real Hyperliquid liquidity, with protocol rules enforced on-chain.

A funded account is a full A-book structure: the trader makes real trades with real capital, and every stage is verifiable on-chain. Unlike most prop firms in the market, prop firms running on Proportion and the protocol itself are aligned with trader profit — their revenue grows with the trader, not instead of the trader.

<Tip>
The trader owns their profit share within protocol rules — protocol rules prevent the prop firm from arbitrarily denying payouts.
</Tip>

## How to Get a Funded Account

Funded accounts are issued by prop firms running on Proportion. Each one has its own approach: challenge, evaluation, or direct activation for a selected trader — this is part of the prop firm's product.

The FA is activated on the trader's EVM address on HyperEVM. Until profit withdrawal, the trader does not need to interact with the contract; protocol operations handle the technical flow. No gas payment is required.

After activation, the FA belongs to the trader — the protocol itself monitors all rules.
